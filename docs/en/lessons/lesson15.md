# Lesson 15: Presentation & Review

## Goals

- Present your completed work
- Appreciate and comment on other students' work
- Reflect on the entire course

## Presentation (3-5 minutes each)

1. Introduce your work's name and concept
2. Demonstrate the mechanism in action
3. Explain mechanisms used and design choices
4. Share challenges and lessons learned
